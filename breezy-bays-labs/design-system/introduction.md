# Design System

This is the public design-system surface for Breezy Bays Labs.

It defines the shared visual and interaction principles behind our products without exposing internal governance, AI-agent instructions, or control-plane workflows.

## What This Covers

- Visual foundations
- Component and pattern philosophy
- Public implementation defaults
- Accessibility and quality posture

## What This Does Not Cover

- Internal review workflow
- Private governance and migration policy
- Agent operating instructions
- Internal tooling strategy

## Design Goal

We want interfaces that are calm by default, precise in interaction, and distinctive without becoming noisy.

The system is designed to help both humans and AI build consistent frontend work with a high quality bar and low unnecessary complexity.
